Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar.
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon.
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeled and sliced.


Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375ºF(190ºC).
  2. In bowl,mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
